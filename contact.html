Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get started with therapy? +

Most clients can be matched with a therapist and have their first session within 24-48 hours of completing their assessment.

Is my information secure and private? +

Yes, we use end-to-end encryption and are fully HIPAA compliant. Your sessions and personal information are completely secure and confidential.

What if I need to change my therapist? +

You can request a different therapist at any time. We'll help you find a better match within 24 hours at no additional cost.
